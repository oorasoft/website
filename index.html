Our Capabilities

Comprehensive Industry 4.0 Solutions

From factory floor to executive dashboard, we deliver end-to-end digital transformation solutions tailored to modern manufacturing challenges.

IoT & IIoT Platforms

Enterprise-grade Industrial Internet of Things solutions featuring device provisioning, real-time data ingestion via MQTT/OPC-UA, time-series databases, customizable dashboards, predictive analytics, and intelligent alerting systems. Monitor thousands of sensors and machines from a unified interface.

  • Multi-protocol device connectivity (MQTT, OPC-UA, Modbus, HTTP/REST)
  • Real-time telemetry visualization and historical trend analysis
  • Configurable alarm thresholds and notification routing
  • Edge computing for latency-sensitive operations

Industrial Automation

Comprehensive SCADA systems, PLC/HMI integration frameworks, distributed control systems, and workflow orchestration platforms. Digitize manual processes with electronic work instructions, digital checklists, and automated quality gates that enforce compliance and traceability.

  • SCADA system design and implementation
  • PLC programming and HMI development (Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Schneider)
  • Recipe management and batch control systems
  • Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) integration

Robotics & Motion Control

Advanced control software for industrial robots, collaborative robots (cobots), automated guided vehicles (AGVs), and material handling systems. Our middleware solutions enable seamless coordination between multiple robotic cells, optimizing throughput and minimizing idle time.

  • Robot fleet management and task scheduling
  • Vision-guided robotics for pick-and-place operations
  • Safety-certified cobot integration for human-robot collaboration
  • Path planning and collision avoidance algorithms

AI & Machine Learning for Manufacturing

Leverage artificial intelligence to transform raw data into actionable insights. Our AI solutions include predictive maintenance models that forecast equipment failures, computer vision systems for automated quality inspection, anomaly detection algorithms, and optimization engines that continuously improve production parameters.

  • Predictive maintenance using time-series forecasting
  • Computer vision for defect detection and classification
  • Process optimization through reinforcement learning
  • Natural language interfaces for intuitive system interaction
